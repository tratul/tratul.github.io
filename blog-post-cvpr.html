Human Detection From Crowd Using Camera Footage in Real Time

  • Machine Learning
  • Classification
  • Feature Extraction
  • Human Detection
  • Camera Footage
  • Real Time

Intelligent video surveillance has got more attention and funding due to global security concerns and an increasing need for effective monitoring of public places. An intelligent device senses and collects moving target information from the crowd for the precise identification of objects.

In this work, we have displayed the after effects of our tests that have been led so as to recognise people utilizing the SHMK technique. A brief summarization of the previous research studies and methods on this topic are given along with a proper explanation of our proposed research methodology. In order to perform the experiments, we collected datasets from reliable sources which are mentioned in this work with links. We have tested Accuracy, Precision Rate, Recall Rate, F-Measure and Computation Time or Processing Time in Seconds, Processing Speed in Frame per Second, Error Rate for the method SHMK on these datasets. The result of every test for each dataset has been collected and documented below. We also tested and compared Accuracy, Precision Rate, Recall Rate, F-Measure and Computation Time or Processing Time in Seconds, Processing Speed in Frame per Second, Error Rate for SHMK method and previous research methods. The results of these experiments are collected and documented in this work. Names of previous methods and links are also recorded here. In the discussion section, the results of our experiments are explained. In these experiments, we found that SHMK performs better than most of the previous methods that have been considered in this research.

Currently have review under a journal.

In this research, we have developed a new method called SHMK to detect humans from a video file. Like every other method, this method also has some disadvantages. Where SHMK is better and more accurate than other methods, it comparatively slower than other methods. The reasons behind this may be a combination of three methods which requires more computational power than the other methods. But as we did not experiment that, we cannot say that for sure. So, this can be a focal point for future works. SHMK method was used here to detect humans in real time. We will use this data to our future research works to detect the human behaviours from surveillance system. Capturing spatial and temporal characteristics of trajectories need to be identified accurately for this research. Additionally, picture extractions from recordings to identifying human practices should be precisely recognized and it should be rapidly and efectively sent in different conditions and start distinguishing uncommon occasions with starting any instructional course. In this work we tried to find out various problems while detecting humans in real time videos. In future we will try to solve the issues we mentioned in our experiment, like low light issue, it affects a lot in our experiment. We will work on this on our future works.
